New User Engagement

My role & goals:The goal is to convert new users to unique bidders. Make it is easier for new users to find lots they like is the key responsibility for my as the UX designer.

Success metrics:

Increase on unique bidders.

Define the problems

I asked 3 questions to define the problems:

  • Where the Majority of new users land? Majority of new users (1.1M) lands on lot detail page but 850k of them drop off, which is the highest drop rate. The second is the auction list page.
  • Why many users lands on these pages?90% of users lands on these pages by organic search.
  • Why these pages have a high drop off rate? Most of the lot of detail pages and page are closed. An auction run 7 days and when users come from organic search, most of them land on closed lot detail or auction list pages. Make it possible and easy for users to explore open auctions is the key.

    • The problem most no-loged in users lands on closed auction and lots page. They are not clear about an auction lot is closed. And they are not engageable as they cannot bid and also do other auctions.

    Ideation and sketch

    high drop-off rates, so that there 1. be clear it is closed 2. have similar lots 3. other lots in the same auction that is open 4. other auction in the same category 5. all category
